Helianthus

The Helianthus (sunflower) is a genus of roughly 70 species pf herbaceous plants in the Asteraceae family, characterized by tall, standing stems, rough hairy leaves and large, iconic flower heads featuring bright yellow ray florets surrounding a brown or yellow dics. Native to North America, they thrive in full sun and are known for heliotropism, where flower heads track the sun!
